Roguelike Upgrade Cards

At the end of each floor, the game pauses action (sets Time.timeScale = 0f) and prompts the player to select one of three random upgrade cards. The upgrade cards system is built using the Strategy Pattern, allowing modular behaviors to modify game mechanics dynamically.

Upgrade Card Architecture

The base class is a ScriptableObject called UpgradeCardSO (found in Assets/_Scripts/ScriptableObjects/UpgradeCardSO.cs). It exposes the abstract method:

public abstract void ApplyUpgrade(Player player);

Thẻ nâng cấp được phân loại thành ba nhóm (CardType) định hình đường viền thẻ trong UI và mức độ ảnh hưởng của chúng:

Mutations Mutation

Card border in UI: Gold/Yellow.

Active mutations change the player's core lướt behavior. To prevent game breaking combos, only 1 active Mutation can be held at a time. If a player already has an active mutation, mutations are filtered out of the selection pool.

Passives Passive

Card border in UI: Cyan.

Simple passive stat bonuses affecting weapon metrics (power, maximum distance, time scales, or combo rewards). Can be stacked infinitely.

Corrupteds Corrupted

Card border in UI: Crimson Red.

Double-edged sword perks. They provide extremely powerful combat modifiers, but introduce harsh penalties to survivability or action speed.

Upgrade Card Catalog

Here is the full database of cards currently implemented in the codebase:

Card Name Category Mechanic & Implementation Details
Double Dash Mutation Enables mid-dash redirection.
  • Sets player.HasDoubleDash = true.
  • If true, players can left-click while in PlayerDashingState to trigger redirection.
  • Redirection consumes 5 seconds of player life time and returns the player to PlayerAimingState.
Vacuum Blade Mutation Pulls nearby enemies toward the player's path during a dash.
  • Sets player.HasVacuumBlade = true.
  • During PlayerDashingState.FixedUpdate(), it performs Physics2D.OverlapCircleAll with a radius of 3.0 units.
  • Pulls all overlaps tagged "Enemy" towards the player position at a speed of 8.0 units/sec.
Trail of Fire Mutation Leaves a path of flames behind the player during a dash.
  • Sets player.HasTrailOfFire = true.
  • Periodically (every 0.08s) spawns a FireTrailSegment collider behind the player.
  • The fire segment has a trigger collider (radius 0.3f), deals 1 damage to enemies touching it, and destroys itself after 2.0 seconds.
Time Harvester Passive Tiers up bounce-kill rewards.
  • Increments GameManager.Instance.TimeHarvesterBonusPercent by +25% (0.25f).
  • When the player kills an enemy while player.CurrentDashBounces > 0, the enemy's timeBonusOnKill is scaled up by this multiplier.
Adrenaline Rush Passive Gives more time to calculate trajectory paths.
  • Multiplies player.AimingTimeScaleModifier by 0.8x.
  • Enters a deeper slow-motion state during aiming (reduces timeScale below the default 0.1f, down to 0.08f, etc.).
Kinetic Momentum Passive Increases collision damage based on physics reflections.
  • Increments GameManager.Instance.KineticMomentumBonusDamage by +1.
  • When hitting enemies in the dashing state, damage dealt is calculated as: baseDamage + (bounces * KineticMomentumBonusDamage). Bounces reset when the dash completes.
Glass Blade Corrupted Massive offense upgrade with high fragility.
  • Sets GameManager.Instance.IsGlassBladeActive = true.
  • Triggers player.UpgradeWeaponPower(0.50f) (+50% launch speed).
  • Base hit damage is increased from 1 to 3 damage.
  • Vulnerability penalty (getting hit by enemies when not in a dash) increases from 10s to 25 seconds.
Satanic Hourglass Corrupted High idle depletion in exchange for freezing enemies on kill.
  • Sets GameManager.Instance.IsSatanicHourglassActive = true.
  • When active, the player's resting life time drain rate is doubled (2s/sec depletion instead of 1s/sec).
  • However, killing an enemy calls GameManager.Instance.FreezeAllEnemies(1.0f), freezing all other active enemies for 1 second.
